Principal Investigator

Chris Kuo, MD PhD

Assistant Professor · Children's Hospital Los Angeles · USC Keck School of Medicine

Dr. Kuo is a physician-scientist whose laboratory focuses on the tumor microenvironment of pediatric bone sarcomas, with particular emphasis on osteosarcoma and Ewing sarcoma. His research integrates spatial transcriptomics, single-cell RNA sequencing, and cell-free DNA epigenomics to decode how these tumors evade immune detection.

Prior to establishing the Kuo Lab at CHLA/CBDI, Dr. Kuo developed deep expertise in the MIF–CD74 signaling axis, THBS2-driven fibrotic niches, and the FragSeq liquid biopsy platform. His clinical background in pediatric oncology informs a translational focus on bringing TME insights into early-phase therapeutic trials.
