Lab Culture

What We Value

🤝

Collaborative rigor

We work together and hold each other to high scientific standards. Data integrity and reproducibility are non-negotiable.

💡

Curiosity over hierarchy

Good ideas come from everywhere. We actively seek input from trainees at all levels and encourage intellectual independence early.

🌱

Growth mindset

Mistakes are part of science. What matters is learning from them and developing as a scientist and communicator.

🧒

Patient-centered purpose

Every project is grounded in the reality that our science may one day improve the lives of children with cancer. We don't lose sight of that.
